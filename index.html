THE LIGHT       NING   THIEF

The Lightning Thief is a novel by Rick Riordan where Percy Jackson, a demigod, embarks on a quest to retrieve Zeus's stolen lightning bolt and clear his name. On his journey, Percy discovers more about his powers, makes new friends and battles mythological monsters. He ultimately learns the truth about his parentage and uncovers a conspiracy to overthrow the gods, which he must stop to save the world of demigods and gods.
